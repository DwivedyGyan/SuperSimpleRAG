The sun was very hot in a small village in Bihar. The ground was dry and dusty. Two best friends, Chintu and Birju, were sitting under a big mango tree.
Chintu was ten years old. He was wearing a simple cotton vest. Birju was busy trying to fix an old, rusty tin tub.
"Why are you fixing that, Birju?" Chintu asked. "The river is completely dry. There is only sand."
Birju wiped sweat from his face. "My grandfather told me the rain is coming from the mountains. Soon, the river will be full of water. We need this boat to reach the 'Secret Island' before the big boys get there!"
The "Secret Island" was just a small pile of dirt in the middle of the river. But to the two boys, it was a place for kings.
As they worked, the air suddenly changed. It became cool. They smelled the sweet scent of rain hitting the dry earth.
"Look!" Birju pointed to the sky. Big, dark clouds were moving in.
Then, the first heavy drops of the monsoon rain began to fall. The dry, cracked ground started to turn dark and wet. The boys didn't run inside to hide. Instead, they dragged their tin tub to the edge of the river bank.
They stood together in the rain, smiling. They weren't just two boys in a village anymore. They were two brave captains waiting for the water to rise so they could start their grand adventure.
